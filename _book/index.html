Welcome

This online book captures the information in my blog, the educational information in my MOOCs Improving Your Statistical Inferences and Improving Your Statistical Questions, and my scientific work in one place. The goal is to make the information easier to find, more accessible to users, and present the most up to date information based on progressive insights, recent scientific developments, and new statistical software.

I have used my right to re-use and adapt my own open access articles, without adding quotation marks or citing myself. This work is shared under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivatives 4.0 International License. Dr. Daniël Lakens
